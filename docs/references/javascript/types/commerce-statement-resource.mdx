The `CommerceStatementResource` type represents a billing statement for a user or organization.

## Properties

<Properties>
  - `id`
  - `string`

  The unique identifier for the statement.

  ---

  - `totals`
  - <code>[CommerceStatementTotals](#commerce-statement-totals)</code>

  An object containing the financial totals for the statement, including subtotal, grand total, tax total, credit, and past due amounts.

  ---

  - `status`
  - `'open' | 'closed'`

  The current status of the statement. Statements can be either `'open'` (still accumulating charges) or `'closed'` (finalized).

  ---

  - `timestamp`
  - `Date`

  The date and time when the statement was created or last updated.

  ---

  - `groups`
  - <code>[CommerceStatementGroup](#commerce-statement-group)\[]</code>

  An array of statement groups, where each group contains payment items organized by timestamp.
</Properties>

## CommerceStatementTotals

The `totals` property contains an object with the following financial information:

<Properties>
  - `subtotal`
  - <code>[CommerceMoney](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-money)</code>

  The subtotal amount before taxes and adjustments.

  ---

  - `grandTotal`
  - <code>[CommerceMoney](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-money)</code>

  The total amount including all charges, taxes, and adjustments.

  ---

  - `taxTotal`
  - <code>[CommerceMoney](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-money)</code>

  The total amount of taxes applied to the statement.

  ---

  - `credit`
  - <code>[CommerceMoney](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-money)</code>

  The total amount of credits applied to the statement.

  ---

  - `pastDue`
  - <code>[CommerceMoney](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-money)</code>

  The total amount that is past due from previous billing periods.
</Properties>

## CommerceStatementGroup

Each statement group in the `groups` array contains:

<Properties>
  - `timestamp`
  - `Date`

  The date and time for this group of payment items.

  ---

  - `items`
  - `CommercePaymentResource[]`

  An array of payment resources that belong to this group.
</Properties>
