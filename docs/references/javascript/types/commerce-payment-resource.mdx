The `CommercePaymentResource` type represents a payment attempt for a user or organization.

## Properties

<Properties>
  - `id`
  - `string`

  The unique identifier for the payment.

  ---

  - `amount`
  - <code>[CommerceMoney](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-money)</code>

  The monetary amount of the payment.

  ---

  - `paidAt?`
  - `Date | undefined`

  The date and time when the payment was successfully completed. This property is `undefined` if the payment has not been paid yet.

  ---

  - `failedAt?`
  - `Date | undefined`

  The date and time when the payment failed. This property is `undefined` if the payment has not failed.

  ---

  - `updatedAt`
  - `Date`

  The date and time when the payment was last updated.

  ---

  - `paymentSource`
  - <code>[CommercePaymentSourceResource](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-payment-source-resource) | undefined</code>

  The payment source being used for this payment attempt, such as credit card or bank account.

  ---

  - `subscription`
  - <code>[CommerceSubscriptionResource](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-subscription-resource) | undefined</code>

  The subscription associated with this payment.

  ---

  - `subscriptionItem`
  - <code>[CommerceSubscriptionItemResource](/docs/references/javascript/types/commerce-subscription-item-resource)</code>

  The subscription item associated with this payment.

  ---

  - `chargeType`
  - `'checkout' | 'recurring'`

  The type of charge this payment represents. Can be `'checkout'` for one-time payments or `'recurring'` for subscription payments.

  ---

  - `status`
  - `'pending' | 'paid' | 'failed'`

  The current status of the payment attempt.
</Properties>
