Clerk offers a comprehensive suite of components designed to seamlessly integrate authentication and multi-tenancy into your application. With Clerk components, you can easily customize the appearance of authentication components and pages, manage the entire authentication flow to suit your specific needs, and even build robust SaaS applications.

Expo apps can target both **native** (iOS/Android) and **web** surfaces, and Clerk ships a distinct component suite for each. Select your target platform to see the components available to it.

<Tabs items={["Expo native", "Expo web"]}>

    <Include src="_partials/expo/beta-callout" />

    For **native** Expo apps, Clerk provides a dedicated suite of components rendered with SwiftUI on iOS and Jetpack Compose on Android. These are distinct from the web UI components — they don't run on Expo web.

    <Include src="_partials/expo/native-components-table" />

    See the [native components overview](/docs/reference/expo/native-components/overview) for installation, requirements, and theming.

    You can also use Clerk's control components — [`<ClerkLoaded />`](/docs/reference/components/control/clerk-loaded), [`<ClerkLoading />`](/docs/reference/components/control/clerk-loading), and [`<Show />`](/docs/reference/components/control/show) — in native apps. They manage conditional rendering based on authentication state and render no UI of their own.

    ## Control components

    Control components manage authentication-related behaviors in your application. They handle tasks such as controlling content visibility based on user authentication status, managing loading states during authentication processes, and redirecting users to appropriate pages. A common example is the [`<Show />`](/docs/reference/components/control/show) component, which allows you to conditionally render content based on authentication and authorization state.

    - [`<ClerkLoaded />`](/docs/reference/components/control/clerk-loaded)
    - [`<ClerkLoading />`](/docs/reference/components/control/clerk-loading)
    - [`<Show />`](/docs/reference/components/control/show)

    ### Secured by Clerk branding

    <Include src="_partials/feature-not-free-callout" />

    By default, Clerk displays a **Secured by Clerk** badge on Clerk components. You can remove this branding by following these steps:

    1. In the Clerk Dashboard, navigate to your application's [**Settings**](https://dashboard.clerk.com/~/settings).
    1. Under **Branding**, toggle on the **Remove "Secured by Clerk" branding** option.
