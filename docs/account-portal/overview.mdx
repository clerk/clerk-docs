# Introduction

The Account Portal offers a comprehensive solution for managing user authentication and profile management in your web application and is the fastest way to add Clerk's authentication to your application with minimal code required. 

![Account Portal](/docs/images/account-portal/account-portal-splash.png)

## How the Account Portal works

The Account Portal uses our Clerk components embedded on dedicated pages hosted for your application will automatically redirect your users between these pages and your application for a seamless authentication experience. You do not need to set up any dedicated paths or pages for this to work. 

## Getting Started  

To use the Account Portal, simply add Clerk to your application and protect your pages. Depending on your choice of framework, you can redirect to them from your application using our NextJS [authMiddleware](https://clerk.com/docs/nextjs/middleware), React [Control Components](https://clerk.com/docs/component-reference/redirect-to-sign-in) or [ClerkJS SDK](https://clerk.com/docs/reference/clerkjs/clerk).
