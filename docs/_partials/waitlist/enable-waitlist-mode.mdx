> [!WARNING]
> [**Email** must be enabled in the Clerk Dashboard](/docs/guides/configure/auth-strategies/sign-up-sign-in-options#email) to allow waitlist invitation emails to be sent to users after they are approved. Support for sending waitlist invitations when **Email** is disabled is actively being worked on.

To enable **Waitlist** mode:

1. In the Clerk Dashboard, navigate to the [**Waitlist**](https://dashboard.clerk.com/~/user-authentication/waitlist) page.
1. Toggle on **Enable waitlist** and select **Save**.
