Account credits allow you to maintain a credit balance for Users and Organizations. Credits are automatically applied to future charges before a payment method is charged.

Account credits can be managed manually from the Clerk Dashboard or programmatically using the Backend API, making them useful for promotions, customer support, and service credits.

## Manage account credits

Credits can be added or removed at any time.

### Clerk Dashboard

To manually adjust a customer's credit balance:

1. Open a User or Organization in the Clerk Dashboard.
1. Open the **Actions** menu.
1. Select **Adjust credit balance**.
1. Enter the amount of credit to add or remove.
1. Save your changes.

The customer's available credit balance is updated immediately and will be applied automatically to future eligible charges.

### Backend API

You can also manage credits using the Backend API.

It supports:

- Adding credits
- Removing credits
- Retrieving the current credit balance
- Listing credit ledger entries

## How account credits work

Credits are stored as a balance on the User or Organization.

Whenever a recurring charge, subscription upgrade, or other eligible charge is created, Clerk automatically applies any available credits before charging the customer's payment method.

If the available credit exceeds the amount due, the remaining balance stays on the account and is automatically applied to future charges.

For example:

| Credit balance | Charge amount | Amount charged | Remaining credit |
| - | -: | -: | -: |
| $50 | $20 | $0 | $30 |
| $15 | $20 | $5 | $0 |
| $0 | $20 | $20 | $0 |

## Credit ledger

Every credit adjustment is recorded in a ledger.

Each ledger entry records:

- The amount added or removed
- The resulting balance
- When the adjustment occurred
- The reason for the adjustment (if provided)

The ledger provides a complete audit trail of how a customer's credit balance changes over time.
