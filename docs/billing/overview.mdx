---
title: Clerk Billing
description: Clerk Billing is a feature that allows you to create and manage Plans and Features for your application.
---


Clerk Billing allows your customers to purchase recurring subscriptions to your application. To learn more, select the guide for your business model:
1. To charge individual users, use [Billing for B2C SaaS](/docs/billing/b2c-saas).
2. To charge companies or organizations, use [Billing for B2B SaaS](/docs/billing/b2b-saas).
