> [!TIP]
> You can let your customers' IT admins set up SSO themselves. With [self-serve SSO](/docs/guides/configure/auth-strategies/enterprise-connections/self-serve-sso), admins of an [Organization](/docs/guides/organizations/overview) can create and manage their own enterprise connection from the **Security** tab of [`<OrganizationProfile />`](/docs/reference/components/organization/organization-profile).
