Use these guides to connect Clerk with databases, frameworks, platforms, and analytics tools.

## Databases

<Cards>
  - [Convex](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/convex)
  - Clerk offers a first-class integration with Convex, the serverless state management platform.
  - ![Convex logo](/docs/images/logos/convex.svg)

  ---

  - [Firebase](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/firebase)
  - Clerk integrates directly with Firebase, a popular Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) provider, so developers can easily add our beautiful Sign Up, Sign In, and User Profile UIs to their Firebase application.
  - ![Firebase logo](/docs/images/logos/firebase.svg)

  ---

  - [Grafbase](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/grafbase)
  - Build applications with serverless GraphQL backends provided by Grafbase and user authentication provided by Clerk.
  - ![Grafbase logo](/docs/images/logos/grafbase.svg){{ dark: '/docs/images/logos/grafbase-dark.svg' }}

  ---

  - [Hasura](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/hasura)
  - Easily connect to your Hasura GraphQL endpoint using Clerk as your authentication provider.
  - ![Hasura logo](/docs/images/logos/hasura.svg)

  ---

  - [Instant](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/instantdb)
  - Clerk has a default integration with Instant. You get all the auth features of Clerk, and a relational database you can use directly from the browser.
  - ![Instant logo](/docs/images/logos/instant.svg)

  ---

  - [Neon](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/neon)
  - Build applications using Neon's serverless Postgres database with Clerk as your authentication provider.
  - ![Neon logo](/docs/images/logos/neon.svg)

  ---

  - [Nhost](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/nhost)
  - The open source alternative to Firebase with GraphQL seamlessly integrates with Clerk to provide authenticated queries.
  - ![Nhost logo](/docs/images/logos/nhost.svg)

  ---

  - [Prisma Postgres](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/prisma-postgres)
  - Build applications using Prisma Postgres with Clerk as your authentication provider.
  - ![Prisma logo](/docs/images/logos/prisma.svg){{ dark: '/docs/images/logos/prisma-dark.svg' }}

  ---

  - [Supabase](/docs/guides/development/integrations/databases/supabase)
  - Clerk embraces the open source alternative to Firebase and offers a first-class integration with Supabase.
  - ![Supabase logo](/docs/images/logos/supabase.svg)
</Cards>
